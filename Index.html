Where does this data come from?

This data is included with the R 'datasets' package. The source is The 'U.S. Department of Commerce, Bureau of the Census (1977) ' Statistical Abstract of the United States.

What algorithm was used to predict?

The prediction algorithm was a randomForest included with the caret Package in R.
